PRIVACY POLICY

PATH PUZZLE GAME

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

OVERVIEW

Path ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our puzzle game application.

We do not sell your personal data. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Leaderboard Data

If you choose to participate in the global leaderboard, we collect:

  • Your display name (chosen by you)
  • Your game scores and statistics
  • Your streak information

This data is stored securely using Firebase and is necessary to display your ranking among other players.

Authentication Data

If you sign in with Apple, we receive only the information you authorize, which may include your name and email address. We use this solely for account identification purposes.

Device Data

We may collect anonymous device identifiers to sync your game progress across your devices via iCloud. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

ADVERTISING

Path includes opt-in advertising powered by Google AdMob. If you choose to watch ads (for example, to support the game), Google may collect certain information including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored using industry-standard security measures:

  • Game data synced via Apple iCloud (encrypted)
  • Leaderboard data stored on Firebase (secured)
  • No data is stored on insecure third-party servers

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Path is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Leaderboard participation requires signing in with an Apple ID, which has its own age restrictions.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Play the game without signing in (local-only mode)

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated revision date.
