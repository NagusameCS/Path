HOW TO PLAY

  • 1 You start from the center tile of the grid.
  • 2 Move to any adjacent tile — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  • 3 You can only move to tiles with a value within ±1 of your current tile. (e.g., from 3 → 2, 3, or 4)
  • 4 Each tile can only be visited once.
  • 5 Your goal is to find the longest possible path.
  • 6 A new puzzle appears every day. Good luck!
